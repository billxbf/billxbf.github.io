Interests & Projects

Tool-augmented Language Model (ALM). How to maintain systematic robustness and efficiency as we introduce tools to LLMs? I proposed a paradigm ReWOO in the direction of prompt efficiency. I'm developing Gentopia to specialize ALMs by tasks while encouraging collaborative sharing, evaluation and supervision. Other topics like quantization and adapter learning sound promising as well.

LLMs in RecSys. Using pretrained world knowledge in foundation models to generate catalog / knowledge graphs, so as to create new recalls and escape popularity bias. Using LLM to mock users, initializing traffic to help cold-start.

Quantitative Models. I built a quant model in 2020 to scan bitcoin buy signals. Later TempoQuant helped me predict Nasdaq dip on 10/13/22 (few believed me on the new bull). I'm recently working on open-sourcing it.

Generative Music. Haven't dive deep but on the top of my wishlist.
